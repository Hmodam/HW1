Arabian Oryx

The Arabian oryx or white oryx is a medium-sized antelope with a distinct shoulder bump, long, straight horns, and a tufted tail

  • Distribution: Middle East
  • Arabian Peninsula:Saudi Arabia
  • Bahrain: Oman
  • Levant: Jordan

An Arabian oryx stands about 1 m (39 in) high at the shoulder and weighs around 70 kg (150 lb).
Both sexes have long, straight or slightly curved, ringed horns which are 50 to 75 cm (20 to 30 in) long.
